about

The participants will receive a certificate upon completing the workshop successfully. This certificate is a Pre-requisite for any couple wanting to wed the Heartfulness Way.

The Vow to Wow workshop is a combination of dynamic, interactive, informative and insightful sessions. These will be presented by eminent psychologists, doctors, and professionals from various backgrounds. All the sessions will be in English only.

The full program will be conducted virtually through an Online workshop format in line with government directives applicable to the Pandemic.

The workshop fees are INR 12000.00 per person. These allow us to bring the best of faculty, innovative design and delivery, certification and a comprehensive feedback for a post-workshop connect if and as needed. The payment options are as given in the registration page.
